Ace Ticket responsive design

During my time at Ace Ticket, I got to see a lot of change happen in the ticket broker industry. Electronic tickets started to become more widely accepted and encouraged at venues. This change in the industry required a more mobile friendly website, something easier to maintain while easy to view on the go. Before I was offered a new opportunity with Iora, I had begun building a new responsive redesign of the website to bring Ace Ticket up to date on new web technologies and practices.
